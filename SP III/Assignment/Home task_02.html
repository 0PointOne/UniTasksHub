Daffodil International University

About DIU

Daffodil International University (DIU) is one of the leading private universities in Bangladesh. Established in 2002, it has quickly risen to prominence in the field of higher education in the country. DIU is known for its commitment to academic excellence and its focus on creating an environment that fosters innovation and research.

DIU offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs in various disciplines including Engineering, Business, Computer Science, Humanities, and Social Sciences. The university is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and a dedicated faculty that is committed to providing students with a comprehensive and enriching educational experience.

Vision and Mission

The vision of Daffodil International University is to become a center of excellence in higher education, and to contribute to the development of the nation by providing quality education that is affordable and accessible.

The mission of DIU is to provide an education that equips students with the skills, knowledge, and ethical values necessary to succeed in a globalized world. DIU strives to produce graduates who are not only competent professionals but also responsible citizens.

Achievements

Over the years, DIU has achieved numerous milestones, including international recognition for its academic programs, world-class research, and a commitment to sustainability.

Student Life

DIU offers a vibrant student life with numerous opportunities for extracurricular activities, sports, and community service. The university's campus is a hub of creativity and innovation, where students can engage in various activities that enhance their personal and professional growth.
